    0x6 AA BB CC DD LA LB TS
    TS:
        0x0 CH: channel number

    response:
        VB LA LB LC LD SA SB .. 
            VB: valid byte 
                0: m is clear
                1: requested range sent
                2: requested range invalid
            0xLALBLCLD: total length of M
            0xSASB: length of following data
